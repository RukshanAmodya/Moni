Section 01

Overview & Introduction

Welcome to Moni — the premium personal and collaborative wealth ledger designed to give you complete control over your financial life. This Privacy Policy describes how Moni ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, protects, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App") available on Android and iOS platforms.

By downloading and using Moni, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App and discontinue use immediately.

💡
Our core privacy promise: Moni is designed with a privacy-first architecture. Your financial transaction data is stored locally on your device and is never sold to third parties or used for advertising purposes.

This policy applies to all features of Moni, including the real-time ledger, portfolio tracker, shared collaborative ledgers, gamified savings challenges, QR identity network, and all cloud synchronization features powered by Firebase.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Moni experience. Here is a full breakdown of what we collect and why:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • 👤
    Account Information: Your email address, display name, and password (hashed by Firebase Authentication) when you register an account.
  • 💳
    Financial Data: Transaction records (income, expense, transfer), amounts, categories, dates, and notes that you enter into the App. This data is stored locally and optionally synced to Firestore.
  • 📊
    Budget & Portfolio Data: Budget limits, portfolio asset types (stocks, crypto, commodities, real estate), and their valuations as entered by you.
  • 🎯
    Savings Challenges: Progress data for gamified savings challenges, including weekly savings amounts and streak records.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • 📱
    Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, used for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • 📈
    Usage Analytics: Anonymous feature usage events (e.g., which screen you visit), app crash reports, and performance metrics collected via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • 🔔
    Push Notification Token: A device-specific FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token used to deliver budget-alert notifications. This token is not linked to your identity without your consent.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Data Type Collected? Reason
Bank account or card numbers Never Moni does not connect to banking APIs
Biometric templates (fingerprint/face data) Never Processed entirely on-device by the OS
Real-time location / GPS data Never Location is not required for any feature
Contacts or address book Never Partner linking uses QR codes only
Camera images / photos saved Never Camera used only for real-time QR scanning

Section 03

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • 🚀
    Providing Core Features: To operate the ledger, portfolio tracker, savings challenges, and shared collaborative budgets as described in the App.

    Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests from public authorities, and enforce our Terms of Service.
✅
We never use your financial data for advertising profiling, sell it to data brokers, or use it to infer sensitive attributes about you. Your money data is yours.

Section 04

Firebase & Cloud Services

Moni uses Google Firebase — a platform operated by Google LLC — to provide authentication, cloud database, and push notification services. The following Firebase services are integrated:

  • 🔐
    Firebase Authentication: Manages secure user sign-up, login, and session management using email/password. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are managed entirely by Firebase's secure authentication infrastructure.
  • 🗄️
    Cloud Firestore: Used as an optional cloud backup for your financial records. Data is stored in Google data centers and protected by Google's security standards. Only you (via your authenticated account) can access your data.
  • ⚡
    Firebase Realtime Database: Powers real-time shared ledger collaboration between linked partners. Only users who have mutually exchanged QR codes can access shared ledger data.
  • 📩
    Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Delivers push notifications for budget alerts and savings reminders. You may disable notifications at any time from your device settings or within the App.
⚠️
Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). By using cloud features, you also agree to Google's terms of service. You may use Moni in offline/local-only mode to avoid any cloud data transfer.

All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption by Google's infrastructure. Firebase Security Rules are in place to ensure that only the authenticated account owner can read or write their own data.

Section 05

Biometric Authentication & PIN Security

Moni uses the local_auth Flutter plugin to offer Touch ID, Face ID, and PIN-based locking to protect access to your financial data. Here is how we handle this sensitive capability:

  • 🔒
    On-Device Only: All biometric authentication is processed entirely on your device by the operating system's Secure Enclave (iOS) or Android Keystore. Moni never receives, stores, or transmits your fingerprint or facial recognition data.
  • 📵
    No Remote Access: Biometric data cannot be used to unlock your App remotely. Authentication is always local and device-specific.
  • 🗑️
    Self-Destruct Protocol: Moni includes an optional security feature that automatically erases all locally stored financial data after a configurable number of consecutive incorrect PIN attempts. This feature is entirely opt-in and controlled by you.
  • 🕶️
    Privacy Vault (Incognito Mode): When enabled, Incognito Mode hides all balance figures from the screen to protect your privacy in public. No additional data is collected in this mode.
🛡️
If you lose access to your device, your locally stored financial data cannot be accessed by others if biometric or PIN lock is enabled. We recommend always enabling this feature.

Section 06

Camera Access & QR Code Scanning

Moni requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes to link with a partner's shared ledger. Here is our complete camera usage policy:

  • 📸
    Real-Time Scanning Only: The camera is activated only when you explicitly open the QR Scanner screen. It is used purely to decode QR codes in real-time and is deactivated as soon as you leave the screen.
  • 🚫
    No Photos Saved: Moni never captures, saves, or uploads photos or video recordings from your camera. The camera stream is processed in memory only.
  • 🔗
    QR Identity Network: Your unique QR code is generated algorithmically from your user identity within the App. It contains only your anonymized Moni user ID and does not expose your email, name, or any financial data.
  • 🤝
    Mutual Consent for Sharing: Shared ledger access requires both parties to deliberately scan each other's QR codes. There is no automatic or background linking.
ℹ️
You can revoke camera permission at any time from your device Settings → Apps → Moni → Permissions. Revoking camera access only disables QR scanning; all other features continue to work normally.

Section 07

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • 🏢
    Service Providers: We share data with Google Firebase (our backend infrastructure provider) solely to provide the App's features. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and confidentially.
  • 👥
    Linked Partners (Shared Ledger): If you choose to link a shared ledger with another Moni user via QR code, that user will have access to shared budget and transaction data only. Your personal transactions are never shared.
  • ⚖️
    Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Moni, our users, or the public.
  • 🔄
    Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections described in this policy.

We will never share your data: for targeted advertising, marketing campaigns, data brokering, or any purpose not explicitly listed above.

Section 08

Data Retention & Deletion

  • 💾
    Local Storage: All financial data stored locally on your device persists until you manually delete it, use the App's data-clear function, or uninstall the App.
  • ☁️
    Cloud Storage (Firestore): Cloud-synced data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated Firestore data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • 🔐
    Authentication Records: Firebase Authentication account records are deleted when you request account deletion. After deletion, you will not be able to recover your account.
  • 📊
    Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics data may be retained by Firebase Analytics in aggregated, non-identifiable form for up to 14 months per Google's standard retention policy.

To request deletion of your data or your account, please contact us at the email provided in Section 12. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region and applicable laws (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents), you may have the following rights:

  • 👁️
    Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • ✏️
    Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data associated with your account.
  • 🗑️
    Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data.
  • 🔒
    Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • 📤
    Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV export available within the App).
  • 🚫
    Right to Object: Object to specific processing of your data, including opt-out from push notifications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.
